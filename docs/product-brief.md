# Product Brief

## Name
Product name: LibreFunnels

Working plugin title: LibreFunnels for WooCommerce

Preferred plugin slug: `librefunnels`

## Vision
Build the free, open-source WooCommerce funnel platform that store owners wish CartFlows was: powerful, beautiful, global-payment-friendly, privacy-respecting, and not locked behind paid tiers.

## Audience
WooCommerce store owners, creators, agencies, course sellers, digital product sellers, physical product sellers, coaches, and small businesses who need high-converting sales funnels without paid funnel software.

## Product Scope
The plugin supports all WooCommerce product types where technically safe:
- Physical products
- Digital/downloadable products
- Services
- Courses/memberships through compatible WooCommerce products
- Mixed carts
- Subscriptions where compatible
- Product variations
- Coupons and discounts
- Shipping and tax flows

## Non-Negotiables
- 100% free plugin features.
- GPLv2-or-later compatible.
- WordPress.org compliant.
- No copied CartFlows code, branding, templates, or UI.
- Works with normal WooCommerce checkout gateways.
- True one-click upsells only where the gateway supports reusable/tokenized payments.
- Unknown gateways get a polished accept-and-confirm payment flow.

## Better Than CartFlows
LibreFunnels should be better through:
- Free feature parity.
- Better visual funnel builder.
- Cleaner admin UX.
- Strong privacy defaults.
- Global gateway fallback behavior.
- Strong developer extension APIs.
- Community-friendly architecture.
